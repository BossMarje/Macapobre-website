BIOGRAPHY

The Early Days & Breakout (1987-2007)

Born Jay Durias Drilon Jr. in Manila, Bugoy discovered his passion for singing at age 7, performing in school and church events. In 2006, he rose to fame as grand champion of ABS-CBN’s *Star in a Million*, where his powerful vocals and soulful style caught national attention. He signed with Star Music in 2007, releasing his debut album *Paano Na Kaya*.

OPM Superstar Era (2008-2019)

With hits like "Paano Na Kaya," "Muli," and "Hindi Kita Malilimutan," Bugoy became one of OPM’s most beloved soul artists. He won multiple Awit Awards and Aliw Awards, collaborated with icons like Regine Velasquez and Gary Valenciano, and lent his voice to hit teleserye soundtracks that touched millions of Filipino hearts.

Artistic Evolution & Advocate (2020-Present)

Beyond music, Bugoy uses his platform to support mental health awareness and Filipino youth empowerment. He released his critically acclaimed album *Lumipat Ka* in 2022, exploring new sounds blending soul, R&B, and traditional Filipino music. He also mentors aspiring singers and performs at benefit concerts for communities in need.
